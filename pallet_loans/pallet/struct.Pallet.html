please note, as bellow:

  • liquidate_token is borrower’s debt, like DAI/USDT
  • collateral_token is borrower’s collateral, like BTC/KSM/DOT

in this function, the liquidator will pay liquidate_token from own account to module account, the system will reduce borrower’s debt the liquidator will receive collateral_token(ctoken) from system (divide borrower’s ctoken to liquidator) then liquidator can decide if withdraw (from ctoken to token)
